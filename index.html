About Apstact

Passionate about creating a Revolution with Apstact

We are building intelligent systems that help communities prepare, respond, and recover faster in times of crisis. Our mission is to use Artificial Intelligence to strengthen public safety, reduce risks, and support faster, more effective emergency response.

We envision a world where technology helps prevent disasters before they happen, minimizes damage when they occur, and ensures that help reaches people exactly when and where it is needed most.

We are committed to continuously innovating and adapting our solutions to meet evolving needs.

Capabilities of Apstact

Apstact builds AI-powered public safety and crisis response systems that help communities prepare, respond, and recover faster. We combine data from weather feeds, satellite imagery, reports, and on-ground signals to generate early warnings, improve situational awareness, and support smarter decisions in real time. From dispatch coordination and route optimization to after-action reporting and performance analytics, our tools are designed to reduce response time, minimize impact, and protect lives and infrastructure.

Products

  • Early warning & disaster prediction dashboard
  • Emergency dispatch & response coordination console
  • Incident reporting, triage & case management portal
  • Risk maps, hazard layers & threat intelligence view

Services

  • Onboarding & integrating local datasets (weather, satellite, IoT, reports)
  • AI model tuning for location-specific risks and languages
  • Operational playbooks, drills, and responder enablement
  • Deployment support, monitoring, and continuous improvement

Core Capabilities

  • Real-time alerts, escalation & prioritized notifications
  • Resource allocation, dispatch and route optimization
  • Situation awareness with live updates and timelines
  • After-action reports, analytics, and performance insights
