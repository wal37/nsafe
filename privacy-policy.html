Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This privacy policy outlines our guidelines and processes regarding the gathering, utilization, and sharing of your data when you engage with our services. It also informs you about your privacy rights and the legal safeguards in place to protect your information.

We utilize your personal data to enhance and offer the service. When you use the service, you consent to the collection and utilization of information as outlined in this privacy policy.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Personal Data

To help with your experience, you may be asked to enter your name, email address, phone number, credit card information, or other details when registering on our app.

Usage Data

Usage data may encompass details such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), browser type, browser version, the specific pages of our service that you access, the date and time of your visits, the duration of your stay on those pages, distinctive device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

Additionally, we may gather information that your browser automatically transmits whenever you interact with our service or when you access it via a mobile device.

Information Collected While Using the Application

During your use of our application and with your prior consent, we may collect the following:

  • Location information
  • Data from your device's phone book, including your contacts list
  • Images and other data from your device's camera and photo library

We utilize this information to furnish features within our service as well as to enhance and personalize the service.

You have the option to enable or disable access to this information at any time via your device's settings.

Use of Your Personal Data

The company may use personal data for the following purposes:

  • To tailor your experience and allow us to deliver the content and products or services you are most interested in.
  • To improve our app and make it more useful to you.
  • To allow us the opportunity to respond to your customer service inquiries more efficiently.
  • To administer contests, surveys, promotions, or other app features.
  • For quick transaction processing.
  • Requesting ratings and reviews of products and services.
  • To follow up after correspondence (live chat, email, or phone call).

Retention of Your Personal Data

The company will only hold onto your personal data for as long as it's essential for the purposes specified in this privacy policy. We will maintain and employ your personal data to the extent required to meet our legal responsibilities (e.g., if there's a legal obligation to preserve your data), resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

Furthermore, the company will retain usage data for internal analysis purposes. Typically, usage data is kept for a shorter duration, except in cases where this data is employed to enhance security or improve the functionality of our service or when we have a legal obligation to maintain this data for extended periods.

Transfer of Your Personal Data

Your information, including personal data, is processed at the company's operational offices and in other locations where the parties involved in data processing are situated.

By consenting to this privacy policy and subsequently providing such information, you agree to this transfer.

The company will take all reasonable measures to ensure that your data is handled securely and in accordance with this privacy policy. No transfer of your personal data will occur to an organization or country unless adequate controls are in place, including the safeguarding of your data and other personal information.

Third-Party Disclosure

Our policy is not to sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without providing users with prior notice.

Our app hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our app, conducting our business, or serving our users are excluded from this policy, as long as they agree to keep this information confidential. In addition, we may release information when it is necessary to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect our rights, property, or safety.

Information about non-personally identifiable visitors may be provided to third parties for marketing and advertising purposes.

Security of Your Personal Data

  • We regularly scan our app for security holes and known vulnerabilities so that your visit will be as secure as possible.
  • We regularly carry out malware scanning.
  • The information you provide is stored on secured networks and is only accessible by a few individuals with special access rights to such systems, and they are required to keep it confidential.
  • Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology is used to encrypt your sensitive or financial information.
  • No data is stored or processed on our servers; all transactions are processed through a gateway provider.

Processing of Your Personal Data

The service providers we use may have access to your personal data. According to their privacy policies, they can collect, store, use, process, and transfer information about your activity on our service.

Google Places is a service provided by Google that delivers information about various locations via HTTP requests. Google Places may collect data from you and your device for security purposes. The information collected by Google Places is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Third-Party Links

At our discretion, we may offer products or services from third parties on our app. The privacy policies of these third-party sites are separate and independent. Therefore, we are not responsible or liable for the content or activities of these linked sites. In spite of this, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

Customer Support and Other Communications

We collect and process customer inquiries, feedback, and communication preferences in order to provide efficient customer support and improve our services. This may include personal information such as contact details and communication history.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and will only use this information for the purpose of addressing your inquiries and enhancing your experience with us.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy may be revised periodically. We will inform you of any modifications by publishing the updated privacy policy on this page.

We recommend reviewing this privacy policy at regular intervals for any updates. Changes to this privacy policy become effective once they are posted on this page.
